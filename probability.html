概率论与统计

从赌徒的困惑到宇宙规律——人类驯服随机性的智慧。

From a gambler's perplexity to the laws of the universe — humanity's wisdom in taming randomness.

▼

发展历史

Historical Development

16 世纪  16th Century

卡尔达诺与赌博 Cardano on Gambling

卡尔达诺在《论赌博游戏》中首次计算了掷骰子的概率，引入概率乘积法则——不过书在他死后近百年才出版。
In "Liber de Ludo Aleae," Cardano computed dice probabilities and introduced the product rule for independent events — though the book was published nearly a century after his death.

1654 年  1654

帕斯卡与费马：概率论的诞生 Pascal & Fermat: Birth of Probability

赌徒梅雷向帕斯卡请教：赌博中途停止，如何公平分配赌注？帕斯卡与费马的通信讨论建立了概率论的数学基础——1654年被定为概率论元年。
The gambler de Méré asked Pascal: how to fairly split stakes when a game is interrupted? The Pascal–Fermat correspondence established the mathematical foundation of probability — 1654 is its birth year.

1713 年  1713

伯努利：大数定律 Bernoulli: Law of Large Numbers

雅各布·伯努利遗著《猜度术》出版，证明了第一个大数定律：试验次数越多，频率越接近概率。确定性从不确定性中浮现。
Jacob Bernoulli's posthumous "Ars Conjectandi" proved the first Law of Large Numbers: the more trials, the closer frequency gets to probability. Certainty emerges from uncertainty.

1763 年  1763

贝叶斯定理 Bayes' Theorem

托马斯·贝叶斯的遗作发表，提出 P(A|B)=P(B|A)·P(A)/P(B)。这个公式成为现代统计推断的两大支柱之一，在机器学习时代焕发新的光辉。
Thomas Bayes' posthumous paper proposed P(A|B)=P(B|A)·P(A)/P(B). This formula became one of the two pillars of modern statistical inference, shining anew in the age of machine learning.

1812 年  1812

拉普拉斯 Laplace

《概率的分析理论》集概率论之大成，提出"拉普拉斯妖"思想实验：知道宇宙全部当前状态的智慧存在可以预测一切未来。同时给出中心极限定理的早期形式。
"Théorie analytique des probabilités" synthesized probability theory. Laplace's Demon — an intellect knowing all present states of the universe — could predict all futures. He also gave early forms of the Central Limit Theorem.

1933 年  1933

柯尔莫哥洛夫公理化 Kolmogorov's Axiomatization

将概率论建立在测度论上，给出三大公理：非负性、规范性、可列可加性。概率论从此成为严谨的数学分支，与分析学无缝对接。
Built probability on measure theory with three axioms: non-negativity, normalization, and countable additivity. Probability became a rigorous branch of mathematics, seamlessly integrated with analysis.

20 世纪后半叶  Late 20th Century

随机过程与现代应用 Stochastic Processes & Modern Applications

马尔可夫链、维纳过程的数学理论、伊藤积分——随机分析使我们能用微积分处理随机现象。概率论深入渗透到金融、机器学习、统计力学和生物信息学中。
Markov chains, Wiener's theory of Brownian motion, Itō calculus — stochastic analysis lets us treat random phenomena with calculus. Probability now pervades finance, ML, statistical mechanics, and bioinformatics.

重要结论

Key Theorems

大数定律 Law of Large Numbers

$$\frac{S_n}{n} \xrightarrow{P} \mu \quad (n \to \infty)$$

样本平均值收敛到期望值。这是概率论最根本的定律——解释了为什么随机现象在大样本下呈现确定性，是保险、赌场和科学的理论基础。
The sample mean converges to the expected value. The most fundamental law of probability — it explains why randomness yields certainty in large samples. The theoretical basis of insurance, casinos, and science.

中心极限定理 Central Limit Theorem

$$\frac{S_n - n\mu}{\sigma\sqrt{n}} \xrightarrow{d} \mathcal{N}(0,1)$$

大量独立同分布随机变量的和，经标准化后趋于标准正态分布。这解释了为什么正态分布无处不在——它是所有随机性叠加的终极归宿。
The sum of many i.i.d. random variables, standardized, converges to the standard normal distribution. This explains why the bell curve is everywhere — it is the ultimate destiny of aggregated randomness.

贝叶斯定理 Bayes' Theorem

$$P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)}$$

在获得新证据 B 后，如何更新我们对 A 的信念？这个公式给出了理性的答案。贝叶斯方法在机器学习、医学诊断和科学推理中日益占据核心地位。
Given new evidence B, how should we update our belief about A? This formula gives the rational answer. Bayesian methods are increasingly central to ML, medical diagnosis, and scientific reasoning.

柯尔莫哥洛夫公理 Kolmogorov Axioms

$$P(\Omega)=1,\; P(A)\ge 0,\; P\!\left(\bigsqcup_{i=1}^\infty A_i\right)=\sum_{i=1}^\infty P(A_i)$$

三个简单的公理奠定了整个概率论的逻辑基础。柯尔莫哥洛夫将概率从哲学争论中解放出来，使之成为测度论框架下的一门严格数学。
Three simple axioms form the logical foundation of all probability theory. Kolmogorov freed probability from philosophical debate, making it a rigorous mathematical discipline within the framework of measure theory.
