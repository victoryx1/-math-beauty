数学全史时间线

A Timeline of Mathematics

涵盖算术、几何、代数、分析、数论、概率、拓扑、逻辑等全部主要分支

约公元前 1800 年 / c. 1800 BCE

巴比伦泥板数学 Babylonian Mathematics

算术 · 代数

巴比伦人使用六十进制记数法，在泥板上刻下了二次方程求解步骤和 √2 的近似值（精确到六位十进制）。普林顿 322 号泥板更是人类最早的数论表格，记录了毕达哥拉斯三元组。
The Babylonians used base-60, solving quadratic equations and approximating √2 to six decimal places. The Plimpton 322 tablet records Pythagorean triples — humanity's oldest number theory table.

约公元前 300 年 / c. 300 BCE

《几何原本》 Euclid's Elements

几何 · 数论

欧几里得汇集了当时所有的数学知识于 13 卷《几何原本》中，从 5 条公设出发，以演绎逻辑建造了整个初等几何大厦。其公理化方法成为此后两千年数学的范式。
Euclid collected all mathematical knowledge of his time into 13 books, building the entire edifice of geometry from just 5 postulates by deductive logic. His axiomatic method became the paradigm of mathematics for two millennia.

约公元前 250 年 / c. 250 BCE

穷竭法与抛物线求积 Method of Exhaustion

几何 · 分析萌芽

阿基米德运用穷竭法严格证明了抛物线弓形的面积等于内接三角形面积的 4/3，并给出了 π 的上下界：3¹⁰/₇₁ < π < 3¹/₇。其方法实质上已触及定积分的核心思想。
Archimedes proved rigorously that the area of a parabolic segment is 4/3 that of the inscribed triangle, and bounded π between 3¹⁰/₇₁ and 3¹/₇. His method essentially anticipated the core idea of the definite integral.

约公元 250 年 / c. 250 CE

《算术》与不定方程 Diophantus' Arithmetica

数论

丢番图（Diophantus）的《算术》系统研究了整系数不定方程，引入了代数符号的雏形。费马正是在此书页边写下了"费马大定理"的断言，引发三百五十年的追寻。
Diophantus systematically studied indeterminate equations with integer coefficients, introducing early algebraic notation. Fermat wrote his famous "Last Theorem" in the margins of this very book, launching a 350-year quest.

约公元 820 年 / c. 820 CE

代数学的诞生 Birth of Algebra

代数

花剌子米（Al-Khwārizmī）撰写《代数学》（al-Jabr），系统给出了一次和二次方程的解法。"代数"（Algebra）一词便源自书名中的"al-Jabr"。他的名字演化为"算法"（Algorithm）。
Al-Khwārizmī wrote "al-Jabr," giving systematic solutions for linear and quadratic equations. "Algebra" derives from "al-Jabr"; "Algorithm" from his own name.

约公元 1100 年 / c. 1100 CE

三次方程的几何解法 Geometric Solution of Cubics

代数 · 几何

波斯数学家奥马尔·海亚姆（Omar Khayyám）利用圆锥曲线的交点对三次方程进行了系统的几何分类与求解，是代数与几何融合的早期高峰。
Omar Khayyám systematically classified and solved cubic equations using intersections of conic sections — an early pinnacle of the fusion between algebra and geometry.

1545 年 / 1545 CE

三次与四次方程求根公式 Cubic & Quartic Formulas

代数

卡尔达诺（Cardano）在《大术》（Ars Magna）中公开了塔尔塔利亚发现的三次方程求根公式，费拉里（Ferrari）随后给出四次方程解法。此役也直接引出了复数的正式登场。
Cardano published Tartaglia's cubic formula in "Ars Magna," and Ferrari solved the quartic. This intellectual duel also forced the formal introduction of complex numbers.

1637 年 / 1637 CE

解析几何的创立 Analytic Geometry

几何 · 代数

笛卡尔（René Descartes）在《方法论》附录《几何学》中引入坐标系，将几何图形化为代数方程。从此"点"变成了 (x,y)，"曲线"变成了方程——几何与代数融为一炉。
Descartes introduced the coordinate system, transforming geometric figures into algebraic equations. Points became (x,y); curves became equations — geometry and algebra fused into one.

1654 年 / 1654 CE

概率论的诞生 Birth of Probability Theory

概率论

帕斯卡（Pascal）与费马（Fermat）通过书信讨论赌博中的点数分配问题，联手奠定了概率论的基础。从此不确定性有了可计算的数学框架。
Pascal and Fermat, through their correspondence on the problem of dividing stakes in interrupted games, jointly laid the foundations of probability theory. Uncertainty now had a calculable mathematical framework.

1665 — 1684 年 / 1665–1684

微积分的发明 Invention of Calculus

分析

牛顿在剑桥瘟疫避居期间发明了"流数术"，莱布尼茨随后独立建立了微积分体系，提出了 ∫ 和 dy/dx 记号。两人从不同路径出发，殊途同归地打开了无穷小分析的大门。
Newton invented "fluxions" while sheltering from the plague in Cambridge; Leibniz independently developed calculus with the ∫ and dy/dx notation. Two paths, one destination — the door to infinitesimal analysis swung open.

1736 年 / 1736 CE

图论的诞生 Birth of Graph Theory

图论 · 拓扑

欧拉解决柯尼斯堡七桥问题，证明不存在一条不重复地走过七座桥的路线。他不再关注桥梁的几何尺寸，而只看它们之间的连接关系——图论与拓扑学的第一道光。
Euler solved the Seven Bridges of Königsberg problem, proving that no route crosses all seven bridges exactly once. He cared only about connections, not geometric measurements — the first light of graph theory and topology.

1748 年 / 1748 CE

《无穷小分析引论》 Introductio in analysin infinitorum

分析

欧拉在此书中引入 f(x) 记号，系统建立了无穷级数、无穷乘积和连分式的理论。几乎每一页都流淌着公式之美：e^{iπ}+1=0、ζ(2)=π²/6 皆出于欧拉之手。
Euler introduced the f(x) notation and built a systematic theory of infinite series, products, and continued fractions. Virtually every page overflows with the beauty of formulas: e^{iπ}+1=0, ζ(2)=π²/6 — all born from Euler's hand.

1801 年 / 1801 CE

《算术研究》 Disquisitiones Arithmeticae

数论

高斯的《算术研究》奠定了现代数论的基础。书中引入同余记号和二次互反律，将此前零散的数论成果统一为一个严密的理论体系。
Gauss's "Disquisitiones Arithmeticae" founded modern number theory, introducing congruence notation and proving Quadratic Reciprocity — unifying previously scattered results into a rigorous theoretical system.

1821 年 / 1821 CE

分析的严格化 Rigorization of Analysis

分析

柯西出版《分析教程》，首次以严格的极限概念定义连续性、导数和积分，驱散了笼罩微积分近两百年的"无穷小"迷雾，分析学从此进入严谨时代。
Cauchy published "Cours d'Analyse," defining continuity, derivatives, and integrals rigorously via limits for the first time — clearing the fog of "infinitesimals" that had shrouded calculus for nearly two centuries.

1829 — 1832 年 / 1829–1832

群论的诞生 Birth of Group Theory

代数

年轻的伽罗瓦（Galois）在决斗前夕匆匆写下遗稿，用群论彻底解决了五次及以上方程为何无求根公式的问题。他开创的群论日后成为贯穿数学与物理的通用语言。
The young Galois, on the eve of his fatal duel, scribbled the manuscript that used group theory to definitively explain why quintics and beyond have no general radical solution. Group theory became a universal language across mathematics and physics.

1826 — 1854 年 / 1826–1854

非欧几何 Non-Euclidean Geometry

几何

罗巴切夫斯基、鲍耶和黎曼分别独立发现：否定欧几里得第五公设并不会导致矛盾，反而开辟出全新的几何世界——双曲几何与椭圆几何。空间本身不再是唯一的。
Lobachevsky, Bolyai, and Riemann independently discovered that denying Euclid's Fifth Postulate causes no contradiction — instead, it opens entirely new geometric worlds: hyperbolic and elliptic geometry. Space itself is no longer unique.

1854 年 / 1854 CE

黎曼几何与黎曼积分 Riemannian Geometry & Riemann Integral

几何 · 分析

黎曼在就职演讲中提出了高维流形和曲率张量的概念，为爱因斯坦的广义相对论准备好数学工具。同年他定义了黎曼积分，精确刻画了可积函数的边界。
Riemann introduced high-dimensional manifolds and curvature tensors, preparing the mathematical tools for Einstein's General Relativity. That same year, he defined the Riemann integral, precisely characterizing integrable functions.

1861 — 1872 年 / 1861–1872

ε-δ 语言与实数的严格定义 ε-δ Language & Rigorous Definition of Real Numbers

分析 · 基础

魏尔斯特拉斯以 ε-δ 语言将极限彻底算术化，戴德金以"戴德金分割"、康托尔以"柯西序列"分别给出实数的严格定义。分析学的逻辑根基至此牢不可破。
Weierstrass arithmetized limits completely with the ε-δ language. Dedekind (via "Dedekind cuts") and Cantor (via Cauchy sequences) each gave rigorous definitions of real numbers. The logical foundations of analysis were finally secure.

1874 — 1899 年 / 1874–1899

集合论的创立 Creation of Set Theory

基础 · 逻辑

康托尔（Cantor）创立集合论，证明了实数不可数，建立了超限基数理论。他关于无穷的层级结构——ℵ₀, ℵ₁, …——被希尔伯特誉为"人类纯粹理性的最伟大成就"。
Cantor created set theory, proved the reals are uncountable, and established the hierarchy of transfinite cardinals ℵ₀, ℵ₁, … — hailed by Hilbert as "the most admirable product of pure human reason."

1895 年 / 1895 CE

代数拓扑的起源 Origin of Algebraic Topology

拓扑

庞加莱（Poincaré）发表《位置分析》（Analysis Situs），引入基本群和同调概念，开创了代数拓扑学。他用代数不变量来区分拓扑空间——"洞"的数目成了空间的指纹。
Poincaré published "Analysis Situs," introducing the fundamental group and homology, founding algebraic topology. He used algebraic invariants to distinguish topological spaces — the number of "holes" became a space's fingerprint.

1900 年 / 1900 CE

希尔伯特 23 问题 Hilbert's 23 Problems

全部领域

希尔伯特在巴黎国际数学家大会上提出 23 个未解决问题，为整个 20 世纪的数学研究制定了议程。从连续统假设到黎曼猜想，这些问题至今仍指引着数学前进的方向。
Hilbert presented 23 unsolved problems at the Paris ICM, setting the agenda for all 20th-century mathematical research. From the Continuum Hypothesis to the Riemann Hypothesis, these problems still guide mathematics today.

1902 年 / 1902 CE

勒贝格积分 Lebesgue Integral

分析 · 测度论

勒贝格以测度论为基础重新定义积分，将黎曼积分的"竖切"改为"横切"，极大地扩展了可积函数的范围，为实变函数论、泛函分析和现代概率论奠定了基石。
Lebesgue redefined the integral using measure theory, changing Riemann's "vertical slicing" to "horizontal slicing," vastly expanding which functions are integrable — the foundation of real analysis, functional analysis, and modern probability.

1931 — 1936 年 / 1931–1936

哥德尔不完备定理与图灵机 Gödel's Incompleteness & Turing Machines

逻辑 · 计算

哥德尔证明任何足够强大的公理系统都存在不可判定的命题，粉碎了希尔伯特完备性纲领。图灵随后定义图灵机并提出停机问题，为计算机科学铺下了逻辑基石。
Gödel proved that any sufficiently powerful axiomatic system contains undecidable propositions, shattering Hilbert's completeness program. Turing then defined the Turing machine and proved the Halting Problem — laying the logical foundations of computer science.

1945 — 1970 年代 / 1945–1970s

范畴论与现代数学的统一 Category Theory & Modern Unification

基础 · 统一

艾伦伯格与麦克莱恩引入范畴论，格罗滕迪克将其推向极致使代数几何脱胎换骨。范畴论不关注单个数学对象，而关注它们之间的关系——为整个数学提供了最抽象但也最统一的语言。
Eilenberg and Mac Lane introduced category theory; Grothendieck pushed it to its extreme, revolutionizing algebraic geometry. Focusing on relationships rather than individual objects, category theory provides the most abstract — yet most unifying — language for all mathematics.

分析学公式长廊

数学分析中最优美的公式，配上直观的几何图解

极限的 ε-δ 定义 ε-δ Definition of Limit

$$\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L \iff \forall \varepsilon>0, \exists \delta>0: 0<|x-a|<\delta \Rightarrow |f(x)-L|<\varepsilon$$

分析学最根本的定义。无论你要求的误差带多么狭窄 (ε)，总存在一个足够小的邻域 (δ)，使得函数值落在误差带之内。两千年的直觉在此化为一行精确的逻辑。
The most fundamental definition in analysis. No matter how narrow the error band (ε), there always exists a sufficiently small neighborhood (δ) so that all function values fall within it. Two millennia of intuition crystallized into one precise logical sentence.

导数的定义 Definition of the Derivative

$$f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}$$

瞬间变化率的精确刻画。当割线 PQ 的两个端点无限接近，割线的斜率便收敛为切线的斜率——导数的几何灵魂。金色实线为切线，虚线割线正向切线靠拢。
The precise definition of instantaneous rate of change. As Q approaches P, the secant line's slope converges to the tangent's slope — the geometric soul of the derivative. Gold = tangent; dashed = secant approaching it.

微积分基本定理 Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

$$\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)$$

微分与积分互为逆运算——数学中最深刻的统一之一。求面积（积分）可以转化为求原函数在端点的差值。牛顿和莱布尼茨各自独立发现了这一联系。
Differentiation and integration are inverse operations — one of the deepest unifications in mathematics. Computing an area (integral) reduces to evaluating an antiderivative at the endpoints. Discovered independently by Newton and Leibniz.

泰勒级数 Taylor Series

$$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n$$

任何一个"好"函数都可以在一点附近用多项式无限逼近。图中蓝色正弦曲线随着阶数增加（1→3→5阶）被多项式越来越精确地复刻——"以简驭繁"的极致。
Any "nice" function can be approximated to arbitrary precision by polynomials near a point. The blue sine curve is replicated more and more accurately as the polynomial degree increases (1→3→5). The apotheosis of "mastering complexity with simplicity."

欧拉公式 Euler's Formula

$$e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta$$

当 θ=π 时，e^{iπ}+1=0 将五个最重要的数学常数融为一体。复指数不过是复平面上单位圆的旋转——指数函数与三角函数在复数域化敌为友。
When θ=π, e^{iπ}+1=0 unites the five most important constants in mathematics. The complex exponential is simply rotation on the unit circle — exponentials and trigonometric functions become allies in the complex domain.

柯西积分公式 Cauchy Integral Formula

$$f(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \oint_\gamma \frac{f(z)}{z - z_0}\,dz$$

复分析皇冠上的明珠。闭合曲线内部任意一点的函数值，完全由曲线上的值所决定——局部蕴含着全局，边界禁锢着内部。这是全纯函数刚性的终极体现。
The crown jewel of complex analysis. The value at any interior point is completely determined by the values on the boundary — the local contains the global; the boundary imprisons the interior. Holomorphic rigidity at its finest.

傅里叶级数 Fourier Series

$$f(x) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\bigl(a_n\cos nx + b_n\sin nx\bigr)$$

任何周期函数都可以分解为一系列正弦波的和——最简单的波叠出最复杂的图样。从信号处理到量子力学，无处不有正弦波的身影。
Any periodic function can be decomposed into a sum of sine waves. The simplest waves combine to form the most complex patterns. From signal processing to quantum mechanics, sine waves are everywhere.

巴塞尔问题 Basel Problem

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = 1 + \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{9} + \frac{1}{16} + \cdots = \frac{\pi^2}{6}$$

1734年，28岁的欧拉震惊了整个数学界：将无穷多个整数的平方倒数求和，答案竟然赫然现出 π²/6！π 从一个几何常数变身为数论与分析之间的神秘信使。
In 1734, the 28-year-old Euler stunned the mathematical world: the sum of the reciprocals of all the squares is π²/6! π transformed from a geometric constant into a mysterious messenger between number theory and analysis.
