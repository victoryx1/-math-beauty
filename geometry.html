几何 Geometry

从金字塔的测量到弯曲的宇宙——人类对空间本质永不满足的追问。

From surveying pyramids to curved universes — humanity's unending quest to understand the nature of space.

▼

发展历史

Historical Development

约公元前 3000 年  c. 3000 BCE

古埃及与巴比伦的实用几何 Egyptian & Babylonian Practical Geometry

尼罗河每年的泛滥迫使埃及人反复丈量土地——"几何"（Geometry）即"大地测量"。埃及人掌握了面积和体积算法，巴比伦人已知勾股定理的大量实例。
The Nile's annual flooding forced Egyptians to re-measure fields — hence "Geo-metry" = "earth measurement." Babylonians already knew numerous instances of the Pythagorean theorem.

约公元前 300 年  c. 300 BCE

欧几里得《几何原本》 Euclid's Elements

从 5 条公设出发，以严密的演绎推理建造了整座几何大厦。《几何原本》是历史上影响力仅次于《圣经》的书，其公理化方法成为此后两千年数学的范式。
Built the entire edifice of geometry from just 5 postulates by rigorous deductive reasoning. The Elements is the second most influential book in history after the Bible.

约公元前 250 年  c. 250 BCE

阿基米德：面积与体积 Archimedes: Area & Volume

运用穷竭法求出圆的面积、球体的表面积与体积、抛物线弓形的面积。传说他临终前对罗马士兵说："别踩坏我的圆。"
Using the method of exhaustion, he found the area of a circle, the surface area and volume of a sphere, and the area of a parabolic segment. His last words: "Do not disturb my circles."

1637 年  1637

笛卡尔：解析几何 Descartes: Analytic Geometry

引入坐标系，将几何图形转化为代数方程。"点"变成了 (x,y)，"曲线"由方程定义。几何与代数融为一炉，这是数学史上最深刻的统一之一。
Introduced the coordinate system, transforming geometric figures into algebraic equations. Points became (x,y); curves became equations. One of the deepest unifications in the history of mathematics.

1826 — 1830 年  1826–1830

非欧几何的发现 Discovery of Non-Euclidean Geometry

罗巴切夫斯基和鲍耶独立发现：否定第五公设不会导致矛盾，反而诞生全新几何——双曲几何。高斯早已发现但未发表。"空间"不再是唯一的了。
Lobachevsky and Bolyai independently discovered that denying the Parallel Postulate causes no contradiction — it gives birth to hyperbolic geometry. Space was no longer unique.

1854 年  1854

黎曼几何 Riemannian Geometry

黎曼引入 n 维流形、度量张量和曲率概念。空间可以弯曲！六十年后，爱因斯坦借助黎曼几何写下了广义相对论方程。
Riemann introduced n-dimensional manifolds, metric tensors, and curvature. Space can be curved! Sixty years later, Einstein used Riemannian geometry to write General Relativity's field equations.

1872 年  1872

克莱因《埃尔朗根纲领》 Klein's Erlangen Program

每种几何对应一个变换群，几何学就是研究该群下的不变量。这一纲领为所有几何学提供了统一的理论框架。
Every geometry corresponds to a transformation group; geometry is the study of invariants under that group. This program provided a unified theoretical framework for all geometries.

20 世纪至今  20th C.–present

微分几何与几何分析 Differential Geometry & Geometric Analysis

嘉当的外微分、陈省身的高维高斯-博内定理、丘成桐的卡拉比猜想证明——几何日益与分析、拓扑、物理相互渗透，成为现代数学最活跃的前沿之一。
Cartan's exterior calculus, Chern's intrinsic Gauss–Bonnet, Yau's proof of the Calabi conjecture — geometry increasingly intertwines with analysis, topology, and physics.

重要结论

Key Theorems

勾股定理 Pythagorean Theorem

$$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$$

巴比伦人、古印度人和中国人都独立发现了它。欧几里得在《几何原本》中给出了经典的"风车"证明。它是黎曼几何中度量张量的出发点。
Independently discovered by Babylonians, Indians, and Chinese. Euclid's "windmill" proof in Elements I.47 is a classic. It is the starting point of the metric tensor in Riemannian geometry.

第五公设的独立性 Independence of the Parallel Postulate

$$\text{Through a point, exactly one parallel line?}$$

第五公设困扰了数学家两千余年。最终证明它独立于其他公设——否定它得到双曲几何或椭圆几何，两者同样无矛盾。几何学不再是唯一的。
The Parallel Postulate tormented mathematicians for over two millennia. It was eventually proved independent — denying it yields hyperbolic or elliptic geometry, both equally consistent.

高斯-博内定理 Gauss–Bonnet Theorem

$$\int_M K\,dA = 2\pi\chi(M)$$

曲面的总曲率等于 2π 乘以欧拉示性数——几何（曲率）与拓扑（示性数）在这里融为一体。陈省身将其推广到了高维。
The total curvature of a surface equals 2π times its Euler characteristic — geometry (curvature) and topology (characteristic) become one. Chern generalized it to higher dimensions.

欧拉多面体公式 Euler's Polyhedron Formula

$$V - E + F = 2$$

对任何凸多面体，V−E+F=2。这可能是拓扑学的第一个定理——它不关心形状的具体尺寸，只关心其组合结构。
For any convex polyhedron, V−E+F=2. Possibly the first theorem in topology — it cares nothing about specific measurements, only combinatorial structure.
