发展历史

Historical Development

约公元前 1800 年  c. 1800 BCE

巴比伦数论 Babylonian Number Theory

普林顿 322 号泥板记录了多组毕达哥拉斯三元组 (a,b,c)，表明巴比伦人已掌握生成这些三元组的系统方法——这是数论最早的印记。
The Plimpton 322 tablet records numerous Pythagorean triples (a,b,c), showing the Babylonians had systematic methods to generate them — the earliest mark of number theory.

约公元前 300 年  c. 300 BCE

欧几里得：素数无穷 Euclid: Infinitude of Primes

《几何原本》卷七至卷九专门讨论数论。欧几里得用反证法证明了素数有无穷多个，并给出了完美数公式：若 2^p−1 为素数，则 2^{p-1}(2^p−1) 为完美数。
Books VII–IX of the Elements are devoted to number theory. Euclid proved the infinitude of primes by contradiction and gave the perfect number formula.

约公元 250 年  c. 250 CE

丢番图《算术》 Diophantus' Arithmetica

系统研究了整系数不定方程（后称"丢番图方程"）。费马正是在此书的页边写下了"费马大定理"断语——引发三百五十年的追寻。
Systematically studied integer-coefficient indeterminate equations (now called "Diophantine equations"). Fermat wrote his "Last Theorem" in the margin of this book, launching a 350-year quest.

1640 年  1640

费马：现代数论之父 Fermat: Father of Modern Number Theory

提出了费马小定理、平方和定理、以及费马大定理：xⁿ+yⁿ=zⁿ (n>2) 无非平凡整数解。他声称有"绝妙的证明"但页边太窄写不下。
Proposed Fermat's Little Theorem, his theorem on sums of two squares, and the famous Last Theorem. He claimed a "marvelous proof" but the margin was too narrow to contain it.

1801 年  1801

高斯《算术研究》 Gauss: Disquisitiones Arithmeticae

奠定了现代数论的基础。引入同余记号和二次互反律的严格证明——高斯称之为"黄金定理"。数论从此成为一门严谨的理论科学。
Laid the foundation of modern number theory. Introduced congruence notation and proved the Law of Quadratic Reciprocity — Gauss called it the "golden theorem."

1859 年  1859

黎曼 ζ 函数 Riemann Zeta Function

黎曼引入 ζ 函数，将素数分布与 ζ 函数的零点联系起来。他猜想所有非平凡零点都在 Re(s)=1/2 直线上——黎曼猜想至今是数学最重要的未解难题。
Riemann linked prime distribution to the zeros of the ζ function. He conjectured all non-trivial zeros lie on Re(s)=1/2 — the Riemann Hypothesis remains the most important open problem in mathematics.

1896 年  1896

素数定理 Prime Number Theorem

阿达马和普桑独立证明了素数定理：小于 x 的素数个数渐进等价于 x/ln(x)——用分析的方法证明了整数的本质性质。
Hadamard and de la Vallée Poussin independently proved the Prime Number Theorem. Using analytic methods to prove a fundamental truth about integers — a triumph of analytic number theory.

1994 年  1994

费马大定理的证明 Proof of Fermat's Last Theorem

安德鲁·怀尔斯经过七年孤独研究，通过证明椭圆曲线与模形式之间的谷山-志村猜想，最终证明了费马大定理——20世纪最辉煌的数学成就之一。
Andrew Wiles, after seven years of solitary work, proved Fermat's Last Theorem by establishing the Taniyama–Shimura conjecture linking elliptic curves and modular forms — one of the greatest achievements of 20th-century mathematics.

重要结论

Key Theorems

算术基本定理 Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic

$$n = p_1^{e_1} p_2^{e_2} \cdots p_k^{e_k}$$

每一个大于 1 的整数可唯一分解为素数的乘积。素数是整数的"原子"，这是数论最基本的基石。
Every integer >1 has a unique prime factorization. Primes are the "atoms" of the integers — the most fundamental fact in number theory.

二次互反律 Quadratic Reciprocity

$$\left(\frac{p}{q}\right)\left(\frac{q}{p}\right)=(-1)^{\frac{p-1}{2}\cdot\frac{q-1}{2}}$$

高斯给出8种证明，称之为"基本定理"。它揭示了两个奇素数间二次剩余关系的深刻对称性，是类域论的先驱。
Gauss gave 8 proofs, calling it the "fundamental theorem." It reveals the deep symmetry of quadratic residues between two odd primes — the precursor of class field theory.

素数定理 Prime Number Theorem

$$\pi(x) \sim \frac{x}{\ln x}$$

小于 x 的素数个数 π(x) 近似等于 x/ln(x)。素数在自然数中越来越稀疏，但遵循一种优雅的对数规律。
The number of primes below x is asymptotically x/ln(x). Primes thin out, but their distribution follows an elegant logarithmic law.

费马大定理 Fermat's Last Theorem

$$x^n + y^n = z^n \quad (n>2) \Rightarrow xyz=0$$

历经350年才被怀尔斯证明。其证明是数论、代数几何和模形式理论交汇的壮丽结晶。
Proved by Wiles after 350 years. The proof is a magnificent synthesis of number theory, algebraic geometry, and modular form theory.
