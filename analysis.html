数学分析

从"飞矢不动"的悖论到 ε-δ 的精确——人类驯服"无穷"的漫长征程。

From Zeno's paradox of the motionless arrow to the precision of ε-δ — humanity's long campaign to tame infinity.

▼

发展历史

Historical Development

约公元前 450 年  c. 450 BCE

芝诺悖论 Zeno's Paradoxes

芝诺提出"飞矢不动""阿喀琉斯追乌龟"等悖论，尖锐地揭示了无穷分割与运动之间的矛盾，直指极限概念的核心。两千余年后才有真正的数学解答。
Zeno's paradoxes — the motionless arrow, Achilles and the tortoise — sharply exposed the contradiction between infinite divisibility and motion, striking at the heart of the limit concept.

约公元前 400 年  c. 400 BCE

欧多克索斯：穷竭法 Eudoxus: Method of Exhaustion

用内接多边形逐步逼近曲线形面积，在逻辑上绕过"无穷小"。为极限理论埋下了第一粒种子。
Approximated curvilinear areas with inscribed polygons, logically bypassing "infinitesimals." This planted the first seed of limit theory.

约公元前 250 年  c. 250 BCE

阿基米德：积分的先驱 Archimedes: Precursor of Integration

严格证明了抛物线弓形面积等于内接三角形面积的 4/3，并给出 π 的上下界。实质上已完成定积分计算——比牛顿莱布尼茨早近两千年。
Proved rigorously that the area of a parabolic segment is 4/3 that of the inscribed triangle. Essentially computed a definite integral — nearly two millennia before Newton and Leibniz.

1665 — 1684 年  1665–1684

牛顿与莱布尼茨 Newton & Leibniz

牛顿发明"流数术"，莱布尼茨独立建立了微分与积分的关系并引入 ∫ 和 dy/dx 记号。微积分的发明宣告了现代数学的诞生。
Newton invented "fluxions"; Leibniz independently developed the calculus and introduced the ∫ and dy/dx notation. The invention of calculus heralded modern mathematics.

1734 — 1748 年  1734–1748

欧拉：分析的集大成者 Euler: Synthesizer of Analysis

引入 f(x) 记号，在《无穷小分析引论》中建立了无穷级数、无穷乘积的系统理论。他证明了 ζ(2)=π²/6，发现了 e^{iθ}=cosθ+isinθ。
Introduced f(x) notation. In "Introductio in analysin infinitorum," he built a systematic theory of infinite series and products. He proved ζ(2)=π²/6, discovered e^{iθ}=cosθ+isinθ.

1821 年  1821

柯西：分析的严格化 Cauchy: Rigorization of Analysis

出版《分析教程》，首次以严格的极限概念定义连续性、导数和积分。用不等式取代直觉的"无穷小"描述，驱散了笼罩微积分近两百年的逻辑迷雾。
Published "Cours d'Analyse," defining continuity, derivatives, and integrals rigorously via limits. He replaced intuitive "infinitesimals" with inequalities, clearing the fog of two centuries.

1854 年  1854

黎曼积分 Riemann Integral

精确刻画了"哪些函数可以积分"。他给出了一个在无穷多个点处不连续却仍然可积的函数——颠覆了人们对连续性的直觉。
Precisely characterized "which functions are integrable." He produced a function discontinuous at infinitely many points yet still integrable — shattering intuition about continuity.

1861 — 1872 年  1861–1872

魏尔斯特拉斯：ε-δ 语言 Weierstrass: ε-δ Language

将极限彻底算术化，给出了"处处连续但处处不可导"的函数，震惊数学界。戴德金和康托尔分别给出了实数的严格定义——分析学的逻辑根基至此完备。
Arithmetized limits completely with ε-δ, and presented a function continuous everywhere but differentiable nowhere — shocking the mathematical world. Dedekind and Cantor gave rigorous definitions of real numbers.

1902 年  1902

勒贝格积分 Lebesgue Integral

将黎曼的"竖切"改为"横切"（按函数值分割定义域），极大地扩展了可积函数的范围，成为实变函数论、泛函分析和概率论的基石。
Changed Riemann's "vertical slicing" to "horizontal slicing" (partitioning by function value), vastly expanding the class of integrable functions. The foundation for real analysis, functional analysis, and probability.

20 世纪至今  20th C.–present

泛函分析与现代分析 Functional Analysis & Modern Analysis

希尔伯特空间、巴拿赫空间、分布理论（施瓦茨）——分析学从具体的函数扩展到无穷维空间上的算子，与量子力学、偏微分方程深度交织。
Hilbert spaces, Banach spaces, distribution theory (Schwartz) — analysis expanded from concrete functions to operators on infinite-dimensional spaces, deeply intertwining with quantum mechanics and PDEs.

重要结论

Key Theorems

微积分基本定理 Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

$$\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)$$

微分与积分互为逆运算——数学中最深刻的统一之一。面积的计算转化为原函数在端点的差值。
Differentiation and integration are inverse operations — one of the deepest unifications in mathematics. Area calculation reduces to evaluating an antiderivative at endpoints.

泰勒定理 Taylor's Theorem

$$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n$$

"好"函数可以用多项式无限逼近。泰勒级数是数值计算、物理近似和科学计算的基石。
Any "nice" function can be approximated arbitrarily well by polynomials. Taylor series are the bedrock of numerical computation, physical approximation, and scientific computing.

柯西积分公式 Cauchy Integral Formula

$$f(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \oint_\gamma \frac{f(z)}{z - z_0}\,dz$$

闭合曲线内部任意点的函数值完全由边界值决定——全纯函数的"刚性"在此体现得淋漓尽致。
The value of a holomorphic function at any interior point is completely determined by its values on the boundary — the "rigidity" of holomorphic functions in full display.

魏尔斯特拉斯函数 Weierstrass Function

$$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a^n \cos(b^n \pi x)$$

1872年构造的处处连续、无处可导的函数，摧毁了"连续函数大体可导"的直觉。数学从此不再依赖几何直观，转而完全依赖逻辑与证明。
A function continuous everywhere but differentiable nowhere (1872), destroying the intuition that "continuous functions are mostly differentiable." Mathematics turned away from geometric intuition toward pure logic and proof.
